About the event

The IASA Aviation Career Expo provides an interactive platform for all to discover all career opportunities from employment to education and training within the aviation industry.

Whether is it is a short term job or finding your new career, The IASA Aviation Career Expo will provide the answers. With so much career information under one roof, this event is a must for all seeking a successful future.
